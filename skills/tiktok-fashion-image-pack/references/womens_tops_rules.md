# Womens Tops Rules

MVP category: `女装上装/外套`.

Supported subtypes:

- `leather_jacket`
- `suede_jacket`
- `utility_jacket`
- `puffer_jacket`
- `faux_fur_jacket`
- `knit_top`
- `cardigan`

Main image template selection:

- `womens_tops_multicolor_triptych`: for products with 2+ sellable colors. IMAGE 1 is the promoted hero color/style. Use a three-zone split: left hero image, right-top same-color styling image, right-bottom compact product-only color options.
- `womens_tops_product_only_to_tryon_truth_split`: for references without real on-body model, including hanger, flat-lay, white-background, pure-background, or product-only images. Use a faceless cropped try-on as the click-driving hero, plus refined product-only proof and construction details.
- `womens_tops_single_hero_detail_sidebar`: default for single-color outerwear. Use a large on-body hero, a same-color styling panel, and product-only detail crops.
- `womens_tops_structure_feature_split`: for clear structure selling points such as pockets, buttons/snaps, zipper, belt, drawstring, hardware, bomber/baseball details, or ribbed construction.
- `womens_tops_material_mood_split`: for texture-led single-color products such as faux fur, suede, knit, PU leather, plush, or fleece when structure is not the primary selling point.

Default reference rule: IMAGE 1 is the promoted product/color. Later images are auxiliary references for color, details, or structure.

Do not include non-sold accessories in product-only panels. Styling basics such as an inner top and plain bottoms are allowed only in model panels.

For product-only references, avoid full perfect AI faces. Prefer neck/chin-to-mid-thigh cropping, phone-covering-face, side/back angle, low-head pose, or other weak-face try-on compositions. The garment must dominate the frame.

Main-image microcopy:

- Default: `likeU · PRODUCT TYPE`.
- Keep `PRODUCT TYPE` in English for brand tone; do not translate it into Thai.
- No price, no sale badge, no long promotional text.
- Keep the line small and brand-like.
