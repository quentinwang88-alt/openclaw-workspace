# QA Rules

Reject or mark for review when:

- Material changes, e.g. suede becomes glossy leather.
- Collar, closure, pockets, sleeve cuffs, hem, or length changes materially.
- Extra hood, fur, belt, quilting, embroidery, or pockets are invented.
- Non-sold accessories imply bundle contents.
- Model hands/faces are visibly distorted.
- Product is too small or core details are covered.
- The image looks like a cheap promo poster.

Allowed minor issues:

- Mild background cleanup artifacts.
- Slight lighting/color-grading changes that preserve product color.
- Styling basics that do not imply bundled accessories.
